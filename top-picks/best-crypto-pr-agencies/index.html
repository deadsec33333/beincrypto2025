What to Look for in a Crypto PR Agency

A significant number of PR agencies have emerged in recent years and newcomers to the space may find it challenging to find trustworthy partners. Let’s explore the key criteria to consider when choosing an agency.

  • Industry Expertise
    Pay attention to the PR agency’s experience with other blockchain, DeFi, NFT, Web3, or other crypto-related projects. Typically, companies have a “Cases” or “Portfolio” section on their websites where you can find all the information about the clients they have worked with. The company should deeply understand all the nuances of the crypto industry to guide your startup to success in this competitive environment.
  • Understanding of Blockchain Technology
    A PR agency should specialize in blockchain, meaning it must understand the fundamental terms of this sector—smart contracts, tokenomics, and much more. This is also a good indicator of how well the company understands trends in the crypto market, which is key to success.
  • Media Relationships
    The responsibilities of PR agencies include drafting press releases and engaging with the media. A good agency should have publications in top-tier outlets such as BeInCrypto, Cointelegraph, CoinDesk, and others. If your target audience extends beyond the crypto community, check if they have connections with mainstream financial or tech media outlets.
  • Comprehensive Digital Experience
    It’s also beneficial if your PR agency knows not only how to engage audiences on platforms like Twitter (X), Telegram, Discord, and Reddit, but also understands SEO, A/B testing, and the whole scope of brand building best practices — this is essential for running a successful advertising campaign.
  • Regulatory Knowledge
    Compliance with regulatory requirements is one of the most critical conditions for thriving in the crypto market. Ensure that the PR agency’s team includes experienced lawyers and other regulatory specialists.
  • Strategy and Approach
    Make sure the agency offers a tailored approach rather than generic solutions. Crypto projects often provide unique tools, and their PR strategy should be customized to align with specific goals.
  • Reputation and Reviews
    Research the agency’s reputation. Read reviews from previous clients, especially those from crypto projects. Check if the agency has any negative cases or scandals associated with their work.
  • Budget and Service Costs
    Compare the costs of services from different agencies, but don’t choose based solely on the “cheapest” option. The quality of a PR campaign can directly depend on the budget.

Ensure that the agency offers the best value for money.

How to Choose the Best Crypto PR Agency for Your Project

Now that you understand what to look for when choosing a PR agency let’s see how to actually do it. It’s not difficult—you can follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Compile a list of potential agencies.
  2. Visit the agencies’ websites and review their case studies.
  3. Reach out to projects that have worked with these agencies.
  4. Organize meetings or calls with agency representatives and ask specific questions about how they would promote your project.
  5. Request a detailed action plan from each agency and compare the proposals.
  6. Analyze how the agency promotes itself and its clients on social media.
  7. Start with a small project or short-term contract (e.g., 1-2 months). 
  8. Regularly request reports on their progress and analyze key metrics. 

Also, remember that communication and transparency are key indicators of a reliable partner.

Conclusion

Choosing the right PR agency can greatly influence the success of your project. As the crypto industry grows in 2025, standing out amidst regulatory challenges and increasing competition requires more than just new and creative technology—it demands a strategic, well-executed PR campaign. 

When selecting a PR partner, prioritize agencies with proven industry expertise, a deep understanding of blockchain technology, and strong media relationships. Look for a team of real professionals who combine deep digital knowledge with regulatory compliance. 

Investing in the PR agency means an investment in your project’s future. By following a structured selection process and focusing on long-term collaboration, you can ensure that your crypto project gains momentum.
